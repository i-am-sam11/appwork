Mood Board

Direction: late-night cinema, not dashboard. Keep the dark canvas Netflix is known for, but trade flat density for warmer contrast, a confident editorial title face, and clearer focal points.
cinematic backdrop
ember — signature accent
ink — base canvas
gold → ember gradient
surface / card
spotlight glow — signature motif
Aa
editorial, not catalog
spotlight over grid
film grain, low gloss
confident hierarchy
tap-first, not hover-first
